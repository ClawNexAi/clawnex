# ClawNex Roadmap

This roadmap describes the major areas in which ClawNex is evolving. Priorities may change based on operator feedback, testing, security research, and upstream platform changes.

Implementation work is tracked in the public [ClawNex Roadmap Project](https://github.com/orgs/ClawNexAi/projects/1) and its associated [roadmap issues](https://github.com/ClawNexAi/clawnex/issues?q=is%3Aissue%20label%3Aroadmap). Completed work is documented in the release notes.

## Shield Validation And Reporting

- More comprehensive and reliable Shield test execution
- Clearer explanations for test outcomes
- Test history, comparison, and exportable reports
- Improved visibility into experimental coverage

## Investigation And Evidence

- Deeper technical evidence for security events
- Clearer relationships between alerts, activity, and affected resources
- Improved investigation workflows and operator decisions
- Better management and review of policy exceptions

## OpenClaw And Hermes Integration

- More reliable model discovery and routing
- Safer selective routing controls
- Clearer connection health and configuration guidance
- Improved handling of upstream configuration changes

## Operational Accuracy

- More accurate model, provider, traffic, and cost telemetry
- Clearer distinction between measured, derived, and unavailable data
- Improved collector reliability and recovery
- More consistent fleet and Mission Control reporting

## Policy And Administrative Safety

- Stronger validation for policy and access-list changes
- Safer destructive operations
- Improved audit trails and recovery options
- Additional safeguards around internal service communication

## Accessibility And Usability

- Improved keyboard and screen-reader support
- Clearer test and investigation detail panels
- Better responsive behavior across supported screen sizes
- More consistent language, actions, and error guidance

## Following Progress

Public roadmap issues describe intended operator outcomes, not committed delivery dates. Follow the [ClawNex Roadmap Project](https://github.com/orgs/ClawNexAi/projects/1) for current status, priority, and target release. An item is complete only after its implementation and documentation have shipped in a release.

Security vulnerabilities must not be reported through public issues. Follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) or contact `security@clawnexai.com`. General roadmap questions can be raised in [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/ClawNexAi/clawnex/discussions) or sent to `contact@clawnexai.com`.
