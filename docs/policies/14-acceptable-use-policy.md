## 1. Purpose

This policy defines acceptable use expectations for ClawNex systems, environments, repositories, credentials, and operational tooling.

## 2. Scope

Applies to employees, contractors, contributors, and any authorized user with access to ClawNex assets.

## 3. Acceptable Use Expectations

Authorized users must:
- use access only for approved business, development, support, or security purposes
- protect credentials and secrets from unauthorized disclosure
- follow documented security and change-management requirements
- avoid unsafe shortcuts that materially widen exposure without approval
- preserve logs, evidence, and auditability when investigating incidents

## 4. Prohibited Use

Users must not:
- use ClawNex systems for unlawful, abusive, or deceptive purposes
- intentionally disable or bypass security controls without authorization
- share credentials casually or publish sensitive information in public repositories
- access data, systems, or tenants beyond assigned authorization
- use production-like access for experimentation without approval

## 5. AI and Security-Sensitive Use

Where ClawNex interacts with models, gateways, or trust-boundary controls, operators must avoid using emergency bypass or permissive configuration changes as convenience features.

## 6. Reporting

Suspected misuse, accidental exposure, or policy violations must be reported promptly to the owner or designated maintainer.

## 7. Enforcement

Violations may result in access restriction, contribution rollback, or suspension of deployment/release activity.
